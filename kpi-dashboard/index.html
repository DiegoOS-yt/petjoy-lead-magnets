The B2B playbook alone opened 19 accounts averaging $3,140 per first order. The auto-replenishment program converted 29 of those into recurring revenue relationships.

The 70/20/10 catalog reset liquidated 29% of dead SKUs in 30 days. 8 vet clinic partnerships became natural homes for therapeutic-adjacent inventory that retail channels couldn't move.

Zero paid media. 100% organic content built on Michelle's expertise positioning. Three posts went viral in the pet wellness niche, each driving 20+ inbound DMs from wholesale prospects.
