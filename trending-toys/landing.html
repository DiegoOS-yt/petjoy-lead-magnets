The Data Behind the Rankings

Real Velocity Data. Not Vendor Guesswork.

The PetJoy Trending Score is built from three inputs: sell-through rate within 30 days, week-over-week velocity increase, and reorder frequency across our retailer network.

A 90+ score means a product is turning at Category A velocity in at least 60% of stores stocking it. A score below 60 is a warning flag.
